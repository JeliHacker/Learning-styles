Carol Dweck Derek Muller
Similarities Both seem to agree that everyone has the ability to learn and that there is a more efficient way of doing so than is conventional.
Differences Dweck emphasizes the importance of mindset in learning. She draws from studies of children in which those with "growth" mindsets believe that their intelligence and achievement is a result of their effort and those with "fixed" mindsets believe that their intelligence is static and unchangable and their achievement a result of their natural ability . Dweck points out that growth mindsets are conducive to increased learning, curiosity, and amount of joy experienced while fixed mindsets are detrimental to all of these aspects. Muller, of the successful education channel on YouTube "Veritasium," explains his reasoning for asking people questions that may make them look foolish.
Other learning techniques Peter Drucker, who wrote the classic management book "Managing Oneself", argues that people fall into one of two categories in how they best learn. People, he claims, are either readers or listeners, and are rarely both.
